Ingredients:
-2 eggs
-1 piece ginger
-2 liaves mint
-1 teaspoon honey
-1 spoon olive oil
-1 teaspoon soy sauce


Directions:
 1. In a hot pan add oil and ginger
 2. After few second add the eggs 
 3. When the eggs are allmost cooked add soy sauce
 4. Add all the rest of the ingredients 
 5. Lets cook for a few minutes and serve hot
